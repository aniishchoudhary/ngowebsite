About Us

Zunj Divyang Sanstha Ashram Training Centre, founded in 2014 by President Raju Hirve, is a dedicated NGO based in Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra, that champions the empowerment and education of differently-abled individuals. With a mission to foster self-reliance and social inclusion, ZUNJ provides practical training in essential skills like mobile repair, tailoring, beauty services, and data entry. Over 2,000 individuals have benefitted from these programs, gaining skills to build independent futures.

Beyond vocational training, ZUNJ extends support through the free distribution of essential equipment such as wheelchairs and crutches and facilitates inclusive community initiatives like inter-caste marriage ceremonies. The NGO also creates job opportunities via NHFDC programs, provides free UDI cards, and offered critical support during the COVID-19 pandemic.

ZUNJ is committed to skill development through tailored vocational training programs designed to equip individuals with practical skills that enable economic independence. Our courses in *mobile repair, tailoring, beauty parlour operations, and data entry* are structured to meet industry standards, providing over 2,000 beneficiaries with hands-on training, professional guidance, and mentorship. Each course is led by experienced trainers and aims to enhance the self-confidence and job readiness of participants, empowering them to take control of their career paths.

Understanding the challenges differently-abled individuals face in finding employment, ZUNJ has collaborated with the *National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC)* to create job opportunities. Through these programs, we provide access to financing, job placements, and other resources that foster economic self-reliance. ZUNJ also offers *free UDI (Unique Disability ID) cards*, enabling individuals to access government benefits, which strengthens their social security and rights.

Recognized with prestigious awards, including the National Youth Consciousness Award, Dr. Kalam Youth Inspiration Award, and Samaj Bhushan Award, ZUNJ Divyang Sanstha continues to make a transformative impact in the lives of Maharashtra's differently-abled community. Through its various social and vocational initiatives, ZUNJ remains committed to uplifting and empowering individuals for a more inclusive society.

Our Services

  • Vocational Training Programs
    Skill-based training in mobile repair, tailoring, beauty parlour operations, and data entry to promote self-employment and job readiness.
  • Distribution of Essential Equipment
    Free provision of wheelchairs, crutches, and other mobility aids to enhance accessibility and independence for differently-abled individuals.
  • Employment and Job Creation Support
    Job placements and funding opportunities in partnership with the National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC) to facilitate economic self-sufficiency.
  • Free UDI Card Distribution
    Assistance with Unique Disability ID (UDI) card applications, giving access to government benefits and support.
  • Inter-Caste Marriage Ceremonies
    Promotion of social inclusion and unity by organizing inter-caste marriages, fostering acceptance and breaking social barriers.
  • Community and Emergency Support
    Relief initiatives, especially during crises like COVID-19, to provide essential aid and ensure the well-being of differently-abled individuals.
  • Awards and Recognition
    Our impactful work is validated through honors like the National Youth Consciousness Award, Dr. Kalam Youth Inspiration Award, and Samaj Bhushan Award, underscoring our commitment to change.

Zunj Divyang Sanstha reaches out to people who are abled differently and offer support to improve their quality of life. Operating under the larger umbrella of Sevadeep, we work alongside other partner organizations to broaden its reach and impact.
