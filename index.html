Our Mission

To empower differently-abled individuals by providing comprehensive support, inclusive opportunities, and advocacy that promotes their rights and well-being, enabling them to lead independent and fulfilling lives.

Our Vision

To create a society where differently-abled individuals are fully integrated and valued, with equal access to opportunities, resources, and services, fostering a culture of inclusivity and acceptance.

Our Values

At Zunj Divyang Sanstha, our values are rooted in inclusivity, empowerment, and respect for all individuals with disabilities. We are committed to creating equal opportunities, advocating for non-discrimination, and ensuring accessibility in all aspects of life.


THE STORY OF RAJU HIRVE
(FOUNDER OF ZUNJ)

ZUNJ Divyang Sanstha Ashram Training Centre, led by Founding President Raju Hirve, is a transformative organization working tirelessly for the empowerment and education of differently-abled individuals across Maharashtra.

Established in 2014, the NGO provides training in mobile repair, tailoring, beauty parlour skills, and data entry, helping over 2,000 students gain practical skills. With initiatives like inter-caste marriage ceremonies and free distribution of essential equipment such as wheelchairs and crutches, the organization plays a key role in enhancing the lives of disabled individuals. ZUNJ has also facilitated job creation through NHFDC programs and offered free UDI cards and support during the COVID-19 pandemic. Their efforts have garnered numerous accolades, including the National Youth Consciousness Award, Dr. Kalam Youth Inspiration Award, and Samaj Bhushan Award.
