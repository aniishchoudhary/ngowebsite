Who Are We?

Zunj Divyang Sanstha is keen in ensuring we reach out to people who are abled differently and offer support to improve their quality of life

We provide wheelchairs , walking aids,foodstuff and clothing to the people in need.

Our Mission

To empower differently-abled individuals by providing comprehensive support, inclusive opportunities, and advocacy that promotes their rights and well-being, enabling them to lead independent and fulfilling lives.

Our Vision

To create a society where differently-abled individuals are fully integrated and valued, with equal access to opportunities, resources, and services, fostering a culture of inclusivity and acceptance.

Zunj Divyang Sanstha reaches out to people who are abled differently and offer support to improve their quality of life. Operating under the larger umbrella of Sevadeep, we work alongside other partner organizations to broaden its reach and impact.
